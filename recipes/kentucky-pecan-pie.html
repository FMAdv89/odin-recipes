Kentucky Pecan Pie

Description

"Rich and gooey, this pecan pie has stood the test of time. Reviewer Ann G. says, "I have tried other pecan pie recipes but always go back to this one when I want the best or want to impress someone."

This pecan pie is a family favorite. I get many requests to make this for our church bake sales.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white corn syrup
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • ⅓ teaspoon salt
  • ⅓ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 large eggs, slightly beaten
  • 1 (12 ounce) package fresh cranberries
  • 1 cup chopped pecans
  • 1 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C.)
  2. Mix together corn syrup, sugar, melted butter, and salt in a large bowl. Add eggs and beat until well combined. Pour mixture into the pie crust and sprinkle pecans over top.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 50 to 60 minutes.
  4. Remove from the oven and let cool to room temperature before serving.
